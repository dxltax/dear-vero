To Vero

I’m sorry this page has to greet you like this. Today was supposed to be warm, maybe even sweet. But I can’t pretend that everything’s fine when my heart feels otherwise.

It’s only been a month, yet something already feels lost. I miss the version of you who looked at me like I mattered. I miss the version of us that felt connected.

I don’t know where we’re headed. Whether we’ll make it or quietly drift apart, I can’t say. But thank you for the version of you that once stayed.

I still wish to see you on the next 24th. And if that’s not written for us, I hope we part gently, without wounds and regrets.

May today treat you kindly, and may peace visit your hear, even just for a little while.

Xo, Delta
